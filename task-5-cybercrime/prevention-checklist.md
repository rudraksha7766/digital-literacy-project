**Stay Safe Online**
>Prevention Checklist

1.Remember, your UPI PIN is only used for sending money, not for receiving.

2.Always check the name that appears on your UPI app before doing any transaction.

3.Use different strong passwords for all your online accounts, and use a password manager.

4.Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for your email, social media, and app accounts.

5.Do not click on any link received in an unsolicited email/SMS saying your account has been blocked.

6.Do not use public, unsecured hotspots for any online transaction or logging in.

7.Always keep your operating system, browser, and antivirus updated.

8.Reporting: In case you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately on 
the National Cyber Crime Portal (https://cybercrime.gov.in/) or call the Helpline 1930.
