Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students


>Do's:

1.Do think before you post; remember that the internet is permanent.

2.Do use privacy settings to control who can see your personal updates.

3.Do engage positively and respectfully with peers and professors.

4.Do share academic achievements and projects to build a professional brand.

5.Do verify information and news before sharing it to avoid spreading misinformation.


>Don'ts:

1.Don't post complaints or negative comments about your university, peers, or faculty.

2.Don't share sensitive personal information like your address, phone number, or ID cards.

3.Don't engage in cyberbullying or heated, aggressive online arguments.

4.Don't post inappropriate photos or language that could harm your future job prospects.

5.Don't click on unverified links sent through direct messages, even from friends.
